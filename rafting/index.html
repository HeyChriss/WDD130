Who are we?

Our trips focus on wilderness, scenic and remote areas of Idaho that are not impacted by industrial tourism. Our goal is to enrich your love and appreciation for Idaho's wild and special places all while experiencing the thrill of active outdoor recreation. We offer a variety of activities and tours to see the great outdoors of Idaho, from orchestrating fly-out river trips involving multiple days to the afternoon half-day challenge of a glacier trek.
